## H1 Portable Hard Drive

Phicomm may have gone under long ago, but the products they left behind are still solid. The H1 portable hard drive is still going for around ¥255 on Pinduoduo. Now that I've switched computers, I really want a portable drive. The convenience of a Type-C port is unbeatable, and the transfer speed is acceptable.

## Baseus GaN Charger + 100W Dual-C Cable

Should I get a GaN charger for my Lenovo Xiaoxin Pro? The Baseus one is actually pretty good — dual Type-C plus one Type-A. The highest-wattage C port is perfect for charging the laptop, the second C port for iPhone PD fast charging, and the last A port for topping up various power banks.
> Already bought the GaN charger — JD.com June 2nd sale price, ¥125 for the charger plus a 100W cable feels reasonable. Pretty close to Luo Yonghao's price.

## N1 TV Box

The biggest advantage is probably the price and the fact that you can flash custom firmware. Mainly I want to try running a software router on it. It's the lowest priority though — I probably won't buy this until I have a stable place to live...
